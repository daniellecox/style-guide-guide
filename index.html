--- layout: page title: OneAPI Design Guide description: Version 1.0 --- ## Purpose To ensure cohesive look, feel and establish predictable mental models across OneAPI tools. This document defines standard style and interaction guidelines which, if adopted, should lead to greater consistency between our tools, and an easier learning curve for OneAPI end-users. The goal is seamless integration of OneAPI tools. These guidelines are intended for use by designers, developers, QA and product team members. ## Contributing info Your evaluation and critique of these guidelines will provide necessary feedback to improve this particular document, and ultimately our OneAPI products.
